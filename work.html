Our Work

Achievements

  1. Year of Establishment: growHub was founded in 2010 with a vision to revolutionize the way people access fresh, organic produce.
  2. Farmers Network: Collaborating with over 500 local farmers, growHub has established a robust network to source a diverse range of organic fruits and vegetables.
  3. Organic Certification: All produce from growHub is certified organic, ensuring that it meets the highest standards of environmental sustainability and health.
  4. Variety of Produce: Offering a wide variety of organic produce, growHub delivers everything from seasonal fruits and vegetables to herbs and specialty items, meeting the diverse preferences of customers.
  5. Zero Pesticides: Proudly boasting a commitment to environmental sustainability, growHub has achieved the milestone of zero pesticide use in its farming practices, contributing to healthier ecosystems.

Products Delivered

As of the latest count, growHub has successfully delivered:

  1. Organic Fruits: 500,000+
  2. Organic Vegetables: 750,000+
  3. Herbs and Specialty Items: 100,000+

Our commitment to providing fresh, organic produce extends to every product we deliver, contributing to healthier lifestyles and sustainable communities.
